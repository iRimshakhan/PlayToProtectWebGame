About Us

“Play to Protect” is an interactive platform designed to help children express emotions, recognize unsafe experiences, and feel supported through gamified tools and thoughtful guidance.

Using mood checks, memory games, storytelling, and scenario-based choices, we gently encourage children to reflect, speak up, and build trust — in a safe, private, and supportive space.

This initiative also supports parents and educators by providing resources that promote open conversations and emotional well-being. Together, we work toward a world where every child feels heard, safe, and empowered.
